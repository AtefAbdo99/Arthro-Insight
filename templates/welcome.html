About OsteoFuture

Welcome to OsteoFuture, your innovative tool for predicting the likelihood of joint replacement surgery in osteoarthritis patients over the next five years. Designed to assist healthcare professionals in managing osteoarthritis more effectively, OsteoFuture offers data-driven insights to guide treatment decisions and improve patient outcomes.

Features

Personalised Predictions

OsteoFuture provides tailored predictions based on individual patient data, including clinical, demographic, and lifestyle factors to estimate the need for joint replacement surgery.

Comprehensive Assessment

The tool evaluates a wide range of variables, including the severity of osteoarthritis, patient mobility, and pain levels, to deliver a thorough risk assessment.

User-Friendly Interface

Easy to use and navigate, OsteoFuture is designed for both clinicians and researchers, integrating seamlessly into clinical workflows and patient consultations.

Development Insights

OsteoFuture was developed by a team of experts in rheumatology, orthopaedics, and data science. Utilising a large dataset of osteoarthritis patients, we trained advanced machine learning models to predict the likelihood of joint replacement surgery accurately. Our model was rigorously validated to ensure precision and reliability, making OsteoFuture a trusted resource for healthcare providers.
